# Contract Generation Skill

## Description
Creates professional contracts, service agreements, NDAs, and amendments. Ensures legal protection and clear terms for freelance work.

## Capabilities
- Generate standard service agreements
- Create project-specific contracts
- Draft non-disclosure agreements (NDAs)
- Prepare statement of work (SOW) documents
- Create contract amendments
- Include standard terms and conditions
- Customize payment terms
- Add intellectual property clauses

## Example Usage

### Generate Service Agreement
```
Create a service agreement for the Website Redesign project with TechCorp. Include:
- Fixed fee of $10,000
- 6 week timeline
- Payment milestones: 50% upfront, 50% on completion
- Unlimited revisions on initial design
- 30-day warranty period
```

### Create NDA
```
Generate a mutual NDA for TechCorp that covers confidential information shared during the project proposal phase. Valid for 2 years.
```

### Draft Statement of Work
```
Create a statement of work for the Website Redesign project including:
- Deliverables: Homepage, 5 internal pages, admin dashboard
- Timeline: 6 weeks
- Acceptance criteria for each deliverable
- Technical requirements
```

## Contract Templates

### Standard Service Agreement Sections
1. Parties and effective date
2. Services and deliverables
3. Timeline and milestones
4. Payment terms and schedule
5. Intellectual property rights
6. Confidentiality
7. Warranties and limitations
8. Termination clauses
9. Dispute resolution
10. Signatures

### Payment Terms Options
- Fixed fee
- Hourly rate with cap
- Milestone-based payments
- Retainer arrangements
- Revenue sharing

## Important Clauses

### Scope Creep Protection
"Any changes to the agreed scope will require a written amendment and may result in adjusted fees and timeline."

### Intellectual Property
"All work product created under this agreement shall be the exclusive property of [Client/Freelancer] upon receipt of final payment."

### Termination Rights
"Either party may terminate this agreement with [X] days written notice. Client shall pay for all work completed through termination date."

## Integration
Works with Client MCP Server to store signed contracts and Billing MCP Server to track payment terms.

## Best Practices
- Always use written contracts
- Clearly define scope and deliverables
- Specify payment terms upfront
- Include revision limits
- Define acceptance criteria
- Add termination clauses
- Have contracts reviewed by legal professional
- Keep signed copies in secure storage
- Reference contracts in invoices

## Legal Disclaimer
These templates are for general guidance only. Consult with a licensed attorney in your jurisdiction for legal advice specific to your situation.
