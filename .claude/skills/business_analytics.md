# Business Analytics Skill

## Description
Provides insights into business performance through data analysis, reporting, and visualization. Helps make data-driven decisions about pricing, profitability, and growth.

## Capabilities
- Generate financial reports and dashboards
- Analyze profitability by project and client
- Track key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Calculate client lifetime value
- Identify revenue trends and patterns
- Forecast future revenue
- Analyze time and resource allocation
- Compare actual vs. estimated performance
- Generate visual reports and charts

## Example Usage

### Monthly Performance Report
```
Generate a monthly performance report for January 2024 including revenue, profit margin, billable hours, active clients, and comparison to previous month.
```

### Client Profitability Analysis
```
Analyze profitability for all clients in 2024. Show revenue, hours worked, effective hourly rate, and profit margin for each client.
```

### Revenue Forecast
```
Based on current pipeline and historical data, forecast revenue for Q2 2024.
```

### KPI Dashboard
```
Show me my current KPIs: monthly recurring revenue, client acquisition cost, client retention rate, average project value, and utilization rate.
```

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Revenue Metrics
- **Gross Revenue**: Total income before expenses
- **Net Revenue**: Income after expenses
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Predictable monthly income
- **Average Project Value**: Total revenue / Number of projects

### Profitability Metrics
- **Profit Margin**: (Revenue - Expenses) / Revenue × 100%
- **Effective Hourly Rate**: Total Revenue / Total Hours
- **Revenue per Client**: Total Revenue / Number of Clients
- **Cost per Project**: Total Expenses / Number of Projects

### Productivity Metrics
- **Utilization Rate**: Billable Hours / Total Work Hours × 100%
- **Project Completion Rate**: Completed Projects / Total Projects
- **Average Project Duration**: Total Project Days / Number of Projects
- **Task Completion Rate**: Completed Tasks / Total Tasks

### Client Metrics
- **Client Retention Rate**: Returning Clients / Total Clients × 100%
- **Client Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Average Revenue per Client × Average Client Lifespan
- **Client Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Marketing + Sales Costs / New Clients
- **Active Clients**: Number of clients with ongoing projects

### Financial Health
- **Cash Flow**: Income - Expenses over time
- **Accounts Receivable**: Outstanding invoice amounts
- **Average Payment Time**: Days from invoice to payment
- **Expense Ratio**: Total Expenses / Total Revenue × 100%

## Client Profitability Analysis

### Metrics per Client
```
CLIENT: [Name]
- Total Revenue: $X
- Total Hours: Y
- Effective Rate: $Z
- Profit Margin: X%
- Projects Completed: Y
- Average Project Value: $Z
- Payment History: [Excellent/Good/Fair/Poor]
- Lifetime Value: $X
- Retention: [Years/Months]
```

### Profitability Tiers
- **Tier 1 (High Value)**: >$50k annual revenue, >40% margin
- **Tier 2 (Medium Value)**: $20k-50k revenue, 30-40% margin
- **Tier 3 (Low Value)**: $5k-20k revenue, 20-30% margin
- **Tier 4 (Consider Dropping)**: <$5k revenue or <20% margin

## Revenue Forecasting

### Simple Forecast Method
```
Forecast = (Average Monthly Revenue × Number of Months) + Pipeline Value × Conversion Rate
```

### Factors to Consider
- Historical revenue trends
- Seasonal variations
- Pipeline opportunities
- Client retention rates
- Market conditions
- Capacity constraints

## Business Health Score

Calculate a composite score (0-100) based on:
- Profitability (30%)
- Cash flow (20%)
- Client diversification (15%)
- Utilization rate (15%)
- Growth rate (10%)
- Payment terms adherence (10%)

```
Score 90-100: Excellent
Score 75-89: Good
Score 60-74: Fair
Score <60: Needs Improvement
```

## Best Practices
- Review KPIs weekly
- Generate monthly reports
- Track trends over time
- Compare to industry benchmarks
- Set measurable goals
- Use data to make decisions
- Identify underperforming areas
- Celebrate wins and milestones
- Share insights with stakeholders
- Adjust strategies based on data

## Action Items from Analysis

### High Utilization but Low Profit
→ Increase rates or reduce expenses

### Low Utilization but Good Profit
→ Increase marketing, find more clients

### High Revenue but Slow Payment
→ Improve payment terms and collections

### Client Concentration Risk
→ Diversify client base

### Declining Profit Margin
→ Review pricing and expenses
